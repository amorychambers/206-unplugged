Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

It couldn't be easier! If fact, YOU don't have to get to the retreat at all! We arrange for a shuttle bus to pick your team up from just outside of the city where you work. This saves on transport costs, so all you need to do is make it to the pick up point and our trusty driver will get you there via the scenic route; bonus views!

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Two words; YOUR TEAM! But more specifically, any teams struggling to co-operate, teams with divisive personality issues, and teams with a lot of isolated folks with few friends or family (who might appreciate the chance to make friends with their co-workers, naturally!) are best suited to the Unplugged retreat.

What size teams can you accomodate?

Unfortunately we can only accomodate teams up to 12 members. Any more than that and our fun schedule of activities will become difficult to manage, and our Retreat Facilitator may have a harder time keeping an eye on everyone to make sure they're super duper safe!

Is there really no wifi available?

Correct! No wifi. But we do have a landline phone!
